# Acme Store FAQ

## General
Q: What are your support hours?
A: We reply Mon-Sat 9am-6pm IST. Outside hours, leave a message and we’ll get back within 12h.

Q: How do I track my order?
A: Reply with your order ID. You’ll get a tracking link instantly.

## Pricing & Shipping
Q: What is the shipping cost?
A: Free shipping on orders above ₹999. Below that, it’s ₹49 flat across India.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Not yet. Only India for now.

## Returns & Refunds
Q: What’s your return policy?
A: 7-day easy returns. Product must be unused with original packaging. Refund goes back to original payment method in 3-5 days.

## Products
Q: Do you have size S/M/L?
A: Yes. Check products.csv for current stock. If it’s not listed, it’s out of stock.
